NICO BIOGRAPHY 

Nicolas Rengifo known as Nico Rengifo was born in Cúcuta, Colombia on April 22, 1999. At a very young age he moved to the notorious city of Medellín where he spent most of his upbringing. At the age of 9 he found inspiration in music and started creating his first melodies. A few years later, after soaking up to his musically inclined environment and studying the great musicians of his era and beyond, he taught himself how to play the piano and the guitar. Music became his medicine as he went through life events where him and his family spent time on the road and refugee camps…
